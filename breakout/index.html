Breakout is an arcade game developed and published by Atari, Inc. and released on May 13, 1976 by Namco.
The objective is to smash all the colorful bricks on the screen and don't lose the ball. Use a paddle to bounce a ball back and forth to catch the ball before it falls down off of the screen, keep the ball in play for as long as possible. You have three lives and each time you fail to catch the ball, you lose one life. Challenge your friend and try to score better than him/her.
